Today's customer sees your cafe on Instagram long before they step inside. A good video ad is a storefront that keeps working even while you sleep. In this article we'll explain, in plain language, which videos a cafe or restaurant should make to drive more sales.

## Why video works for cafes and restaurants

Food and drink are inherently visual. Steam rising off a coffee, a fork cutting into cake, the sizzle of meat on the grill — none of it fits into a still photo. Video carries that feeling, and that feeling makes people hungry.

On top of that, the Instagram and TikTok algorithms push video content to more people. So the right Reel can multiply how many people discover you.

## What videos should you make?

You don't need every format at once. Start with a few simple templates:

### 1. Menu highlight video

Show your best-selling items in a short, colorful Reel. Focus on one product so the message doesn't get lost.

### 2. Behind the scenes

People love seeing the humans behind the brand. Prepping a pour-over or plating a dish builds trust.

### 3. Real customer review

One honest sentence from a happy customer is more persuasive than dozens of ads.

## The secret to a Reel that gets seen

The first second is everything. If you don't grab the viewer in the first 3 seconds, they scroll. Open with a tight, mouth-watering shot — not your logo.

Keep the rhythm fast, add captions (many people watch on mute), and use trending audio. And never forget the call to action: "Book now" or "Address in bio."

## Where to start?

You don't need a big budget. Start with a small monthly package of a few professional videos, measure the results, then scale. Consistency is what matters most.
