Great motion graphics blend rhythm, timing, and story.

## Timing is everything

Natural movement comes from easing curves that accelerate and decelerate.

```js
// easing example in GSAP
gsap.to(".box", { x: 300, duration: 1, ease: "power3.out" })
```

Respect these principles and your work will look truly professional.
