Video is the most engaging content format on social platforms. In this article we explore why investing in video delivers the highest ROI for brands.

## Video drives sales

After watching a good video, audiences are far more likely to buy. Video lets you show a product's value in seconds.

### Three key points

1. **The hook:** the first three seconds decide everything.
2. **Narrative:** tell a story, not just a feature list.
3. **Call to action:** guide the viewer to the next step.

> A great video creates emotion — and emotion drives the buying decision.
